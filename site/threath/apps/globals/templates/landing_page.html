Getting friends together has never been easier

Get your friends together with free, private, one-on-one and group chat. Want to get together with friends for a bite or a night on the town? You can find a nearby restaurant or bar and share the details to your friends, right in the app. You can even find a good movie to see, and buy tickets without ever leaving the chat with your friends.

Group chat, evolved

All the features you've come to expect in a great group messaging app, and more: One-on-one and group chat, photo and video sharing, plus sharing files and documents from your phone, tablet, desktop computer or Dropbox. The most complete restaurant and movie integration in a messaging app ever. Fun emoticons and stickers to spice up the conversation.
